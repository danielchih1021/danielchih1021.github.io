Design Principles - Daniel Chih

White Space

Nike's Site

In the merchandize category row on the top of the page, there's enough white space to really have the categories stand out. White space makes everything within visually stand out to users. That helps accomplish the primary purpose of the website.

Visual Hierarchy

Purple's Site

Purple's site's visual hierarchy is visually obvious with the priority order: 1. Mattress Types , 2. its brand name and 3. The customer satisfaction rate. Through guiding customer's attention in hierarchy, the sales of the mattress can be maximized.

Alignment

Tesla's Site

Tesla's page has alignment on the model selection and the order/inventory options with some visual hierarchy on the big picture of the car in the middle and contrast on the colors of the customer order and existing inventory buttons as well.
